Four levels of meticulously detailed living — from a sun-lit chef's kitchen to a private attic retreat. Every room at 28 Bristol Road is proportioned to feel generous, purposeful, and undeniably refined.

Formal living with Napoleon gas fireplace, Calacatta Viola Honed stone surround. 3-piece crown molding, White Oak arch details.

Primary Suite

18′ × 12′ + Sitting

Second floor. Private 17′ × 10′ sitting area, dual walk-in closets (Sangiacomo), Napoleon gas fireplace, spa bathroom.

Bedroom 1 (First Floor)

17.5′ × 10.5′

First-floor guest suite with en-suite bath and direct outdoor access. Ideal for in-law or au-pair.

Bedroom 3

17.5′ × 13.5′

Generous second-floor room with Kallista One en-suite, Sangiacomo closet, and natural light from dual exposures.

Structure

Stone · Wood · Glass
